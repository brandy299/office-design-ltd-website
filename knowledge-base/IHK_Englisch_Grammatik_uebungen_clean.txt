Niagara Falls (Niagarafälle)
Niagara Falls is a group of waterfalls on the border between the United States and Canada. The Falls are a POPULAR
(popular) tourist attraction. The Niagara River drops here and although the Falls are not DEEP (deep), they are very
WIDE (wide) and the amount of water is ENORMOUS (enormous). So it is very NOISY (noisy) near the Falls. That's
why the Indian tribe (Indianerstamm), who used to live here, called the falls Niagara (meaning “thunder of waters”).
Niagara Falls form a NATURAL (natural) border between the USA and Canada. Tourists come here
FREQUENTLY (frequent).  Many years ago, an Indian tribe lived here PEACEFULLY (peaceful).
