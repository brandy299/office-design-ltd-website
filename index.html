About Office Design Ltd.

Our Brand Identity — Click to Enlarge

Office Design Ltd. is a British company that designs and sells premium ergonomic office furniture. We are a limited company (Ltd.) registered in the United Kingdom.

Our headquarters are in London, and we have a branch office in Düsseldorf, Germany. We supply furniture to businesses across Europe, from small start-ups to large corporate headquarters.

Our Mission

We create office furniture that looks great and prioritizes ergonomics and employee health. Healthy employees are productive employees.

  • ● Business areas: Office chairs, desks, and storage solutions
  • ● Clients: Start-ups, agencies, and Fortune 500 companies
  • ● Markets: UK, Germany, and the rest of Europe
  • ● Focus: B2B (Business-to-Business) sales
