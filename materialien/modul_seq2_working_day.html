Describing Your Daily Tasks

When you talk or write about your job, these phrases help you sound professional and clear.

Key Phrases

I am responsible for... describing your main duties
My main tasks include... listing several activities
I deal with... what/who you work with
I spend a lot of time... activities that take most of your day
I also help with... secondary tasks
Every day I... daily routines

Example: A Day in the Life

Read how Helen Carter describes her typical day. Notice the phrases she uses.
